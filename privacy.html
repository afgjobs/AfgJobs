Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AfgJobs collects, uses, and stores information when you use this website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you enter, such as full name, email, phone, and password.
  • Job post details you submit, such as title, description, category, location, budget, contact, and optional media.
  • Feedback and newsletter email entries you submit through forms.
  • Basic usage preferences, such as selected theme mode.

2. How We Use Information

  • To show your account status and support sign-in features.
  • To display posted jobs and related details.
  • To store feedback and newsletter subscriptions.
  • To improve site usability and user experience.

3. Data Storage

In the current version of this site, form data and account data are stored in your browser local storage on your own device. This means data is not synced across devices by default.

4. Data Sharing

AfgJobs does not sell personal data. We only show data you choose to publish in job posts (such as contact details).

5. Security Notice

No browser-based system is fully secure. Avoid entering highly sensitive personal information in public fields.

6. Your Choices

  • You can update or remove job posts and profile data from your device.
  • You can clear browser local storage to remove locally stored site data.
  • You can contact us to request clarification about this policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Material changes should be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact page.
